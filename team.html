Communications & Design
Roger Rybkowski
Parish Communications Advisor · Seton Collaborative
35 Years Experience Catholic Institutions Brand & Design Multi-Channel Strategy Adobe Creative Suite U.S. Navy Veteran

For 35 years, Roger Rybkowski built communications programs for Catholic schools and parishes across California — running his own design and communications firm for 25 years, then serving a decade as Director of Marketing and Communications at Palma School in Salinas. He knows Catholic institutional life from the inside: the pastor's authority, the staff's constraints, the volunteer culture, the parishioner who needs to feel invited rather than announced at. He has carried an institution's entire communications identity in his hands — alone — and produced work that moved people.

He and his wife Cindy recently relocated to Connecticut to be closer to their family. When he arrived, he reached out to the Diocese and asked if he could help. That's who Roger is. He didn't answer a job posting. He showed up and offered. His approach to multi-channel engagement — understanding how a direct mail piece, a well-designed bulletin, a digital post, and a website work together as a system rather than as separate tasks — is something most parishes have never had applied to their specific situation. He brings that to every audit and every strategy session he leads.
