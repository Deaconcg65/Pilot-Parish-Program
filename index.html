Why This Exists

Every Parish Deserves Professional Support

The Diocese of Bridgeport has identified parishes that would benefit most from focused, hands-on support. The Pilot Parish Initiative is how that support is delivered — not through a program mailed from a chancery office, but through a dedicated team that comes in, understands the specific parish, and builds something lasting.

This is not a consulting engagement. It is not a checklist. It is an ongoing relationship between the Diocese and the Pilot Parishes — with real accountability and real results.

How It Works

Four Steps. One Clear Path.

Every parish engagement follows the same sequence — understand first, prescribe second, build third, sustain always.

01

Assessment

We examine where the parish actually stands — in each area of support. No assumptions. We document what exists, what's missing, and where the highest-leverage opportunities are.

02

Conversation

Before recommending anything, we sit with the pastor and key staff. Findings are presented clearly. The path forward is built together — not handed down from outside.

03

Execution

Seton handles the work. Website builds, technology remediation, reporting tools, ministry strategy — delivered by specialists on a timeline that respects parish life.

04

Ongoing Support

Managed services, on-demand training, periodic reviews. The relationship continues so the parish doesn't lose momentum when the initial engagement ends.
