Why This Exists

Every Parish Deserves Professional Support

Most pilot parishes are doing their best with what they have. What they often lack is not commitment or faith — it is access to the kind of communications expertise, technology management, data insight, and youth ministry strategy that larger institutions take for granted.

The Pilot Parish Initiative exists to close that gap. Not with a program delivered from a distance, but with a dedicated team that comes alongside each parish, understands their specific situation, and delivers support that is built for them.

This is not a consulting engagement. It is not a checklist. It is an ongoing relationship between the Diocese and the Pilot Parishes — with real people, doing real work, in service of the mission.

The Process

How the Initiative Works

Every parish engagement follows the same sequence. The details vary by area and by what the assessment reveals — but the approach is always the same: understand first, prescribe second, execute third.

01

Assessment

We come in and look honestly at where the parish stands — in each area of support. No assumptions. We document what exists, what's missing, and what the highest-leverage opportunities are.

02

Conversation

Before we recommend anything, we sit down with the pastor and key staff. The findings are presented clearly. The path forward is built together — not handed down.

03

Execution

Seton handles the work. Communications builds, technology remediation, reporting tools, ministry strategy — delivered by people who know what they're doing, on a timeline that respects parish life.

04

Ongoing Support

The relationship continues after the initial engagement. Managed services, on-demand training, periodic reviews — so the parish doesn't lose momentum when the team moves on.

For Pastors

What to Expect as a Pilot Parish

How Engagement Begins

A member of the Intervention Team will reach out directly to schedule an initial conversation — not a presentation, a conversation. We want to understand your parish before we show you anything. From there, we propose a sequence of support based on where your greatest needs are.

Learn about the process →

What We Ask of You

Time with the pastor for an initial conversation. Access to staff for relevant assessments. Honest feedback on what's working and what isn't. We do the work — but we need the parish's knowledge of itself to do it well. This is a partnership, not a delivery.

Contact the team →
Our Commitment

We will never overstate what we've found, automate something that requires human judgment, or recommend something that creates more burden than it relieves. Credibility with your pastor and your staff is worth more to us than a quick result. We are here for the long engagement — not the impressive first impression.
